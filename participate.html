Ways YOU can make a difference

There are so many ways that we as people can make a difference and can contribute to the cause. Some ways include attending protests, signing petitions, calling/emailing government officials, and donating towards different organizations. Before saying that you support the movement, you must show it. Hover over the pictures to see ways you can make a difference!

There are many protests that are happening all around the country, and you can definitely be a part of it.
There are many petitions and ways to get in contact with your government officials. Some petitions to sign are Justice for George Floyd, Justice for Brianna Taylor, and Justice for Ahmaud Arbery. All these petitions can be found on change.org. You can also call DA Mike Freeman in Minnesotta at 612-348-5550 , text FLOYD to 55-156, text JUSTICE to 66-8336, text ENOUGH to 55-165 and leave a message to Louisville Mayor and demand justice for Breonna Taylor at 502-574-2003.
There are many organizations that you can donate to such as the George Floyd Memorial Fund, Minnesota Freedom Fund, Reclaim the Block, National Bail Out, Black Lives Matter, Bail Project, Black Visions Collective, Campaign Zero, National Bail Fund Network, The Innocent Project, Run with Maud and Justice for Breonna.
